About

Unlock the full potential of Google Maps location sharing service with Gmap24, your ultimate solution for comprehensive location data analysis and tracking. Elevate your experience and delve into the world of location insights effortlessly with this powerful desktop application.

Securely store your data on your computer, enabling you to extract valuable insights and make informed decisions. Discover the most visited places, view movement trajectories, and gain a deeper understanding of location data with Gmap24.

  • Ability to choose the timeframe for analysis
